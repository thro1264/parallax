Top 5
Greatest Colorado Buffaloes of All Time

01
Rashaan Salaam

Rashaan Salaam played running back for the Colorado Buffaloes from 1992 to 1994. During his 1994 season he ran for a school-record 2,055 yards and 24 touchdowns. He was also essential in the Buffs 11-1 record and their victory against Norte Dame in the Fiesta Bowl. That year he was a unanimous first-team All-American and the winner of the Heisman Trophy.

02
Eric Bieniemy

Eric Bieniemy played running back for the Colorado Buffaloes football team from 1988 to 1991. During his 1990 season he was the nation's second leading rusher with 1628 yards along with 17 touchdowns. Additionally, he finished third in the Heisman Trophy ballet that season. He finished his college career as Colorado's all time leader in rushing, all purpose yards, and touchdowns.

03
Darian Hagan

Darian Hagan played quarterback for the Colorado Buffaloes from 1988 to 1991. He took over for Sal Aunese his sophmore season, becoming the sixth player in the history of NCAAA Division I-A football to gain more than 1,000 yards passing and rushing in the same season. He finished his CU career with 3,801 passing yards and 2,007 rushing yards.

04
Alfred Williams

Alfred Williams played linebacker for the Colorado Buffaloes from 1988 to 1991. He was a unanimous All-American pick in 1990, a consensus All-American in 1989, and the 1990 Butkus Award Winner. He was the captain of the 1990 Colorado National Championship team. He finished his career with 263 tackles and 35 sacks. In 2010, he was inducted into College Football Hall of Fame.

05
Cliff Branch

Cliff Branch was both a wide receiver and a sprinter on the track team for the Colorado Buffaloes from 1968 to 1971. He was a first team All-American his senior year and was a pivotal role in thei 10-2 record that season. After his college career, he won three Super Bowls with the Raiders and was a 4 time Pro Bowler and was a 3 time First Team All-Pro player.
